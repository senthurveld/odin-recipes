Medu Vada Recipe

Description

Medu Vada Recipe, Indian crispy doughnut shaped dal fritters. Most popular South Indian snack made with whole urad dal which is soaked and ground into a batter.It is shaped and fried which turn out crispy on the outside and fluffy inside

Ingredients

  • Urad Dal
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Green Chillies
  • Ginger
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Curry Leaves
  • Onions

Method

  1. Add 2 cup urad dal to a large bowl wash it well. Add fresh water and soak it for 4 to 5 hrs.

  2. After hours dal well soaked, discard the water completely from dal and rinse.

  3. Add the dal to a grinder or blender jar along with 1/3 teaspoon salt.

  4. Sprinkle 3 tablespoons ice cold water to start off. Blend for a short time about 25 to 30 seconds.

  5. Grind till the batter looks fluffy, thick, lightly aerated and white in color.

    6. (Tips: Check if the batter is done correctly. Take a small bowl filled with water. Take little batter and form a ball, drop it in the water. If the consistency & texture of the batter is light, the batter floats well.The batter will not float if the consistency is very thin. Read the notes below what to do if the batter becomes runny/thin.)

  6. Add a tiny pinch of baking soda, half teaspoon crushed pepper, 1 to 2 chopped green chilies, 1 tablespoon fine chopped ginger, 1 sprig curry leaves and 2 to 3 tbsps chopped onion or fresh coconut.Mix the batter well.

  7. On a medium flame, heat oil in a deep pan. Take small portions of this batter to your fingers. Make a ball. Slightly flatten the ball with your thumb. Next make a hole in the center.

  8. Face your palm towards the oil, drop in the hot oil by shaking your fingers gently. Take care. Drop these just by shaking your fingers.

  9. Fry medu vada on a medium flame till they turn golden & crisp.
