Bread Omelette Recipe

Description

Bread Omelette is a universal favourite breakfast and a snack made by toasting bread with beaten eggs, onions, spices & herbs. Bread Omlette is a filling & quick breakfast and a nice anytime snack.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Breag
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Onions, Veggies, Coriander leaves (optional)

Method

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl until frothy.

  2. Add chopped onions, chilies, black peppers, salt & coriander leaves.

  3. Heat a pan with oil on a medium flame. When the pan turns hot, pour the beaten eggs. When the mixture begins to firm up shape to a square.

  4. Flip it and fry on a medium heat until the egg is cooked completely. Sprinkle black pepper.

  5. Place a bread slice on the pan and transfer the egg omelet to the bread. Cover it with another slice of bread.

  6. Flip and toast it on both the sides on a low flame.
